What To Expect

It's not easy starting out a new Journey. Find out what Place of Rest offers...

Service

The main celebration runs for about an 1hour 20mins and includes a time of singing, prayer and an encouraging message.

Where do I park?

If you are planning to attend one of our Sunday morning services, you need not worry about parking. We have several spots reserved just for first-time visitors.

What do I wear?

Dress comfortably, we're a church of diverse people. You'll feel right at home wearing shorts, to jeans to smart casual.

What about my kids?

The Children's CHurch runs every Sunday. We have loads of activities planned out for different age groups.

Is there a mother’s area?

Yes, we have reserved seats for mums to take their children, but also to sit with newborns during the service. We are a family and want you, as mums to be a part of what is going on as much as possible.

Statement of Faith

God the Father

Creator of Heaven and Earth, who made man in His own image and likeness.

The Holy Trinity

The three persons of the Godhead: God the Father, God the Son, and God the Holy Spirit Three in One.

The Virgin Birth

Jesus was conceived by the Holy Spirit and born of the Virgin Mary.

The Holy Spirit

The Comforter and Teacher of all things who dwell within us, uniting us to Jesus Christ.

The Death and Resurrection of Jesus Christ

Jesus was crucified and died for the sins of mankind on the cross at Calvary, was buried and rose from the dead on the third day. He ascended into Heaven and is forever seated at the right hand of God the Father.

The Blood of Jesus Christ

It was shed for us on the cross at Calvary, cleanses us from all sin and grants us access into the presence of God.

Vision & Mission

Vision

To reach all people for Jesus Christ. To empower them by the word of God to attain purpose. To impact people in every spheres of life for Christ with heaven in focus.

Mission

  • Create an environment where the unsaved of all ages, race, status can seek and find Jesus Christ without being judged.
  • Provide easy access to uncompromised Word of God.
  • Develop and strengthen the pursuit of deeper relationship with Jesus Christ in an atmosphere of love

Our Roots


Redeemed Christian Church of God ( RCCG) has its humble beginnings in Africa (Nigeria) through an encounter of one man with God. That man was Josiah O Akindayomi. Born not only into an idolatrous background but was surrounded by idol worshipers just like the patriarch, Abraham. But he was determined to please God and not partake of such abomination. This relentless pursue of righteousness lead him to encounter God. This encounter changed the course of his life for good. 


Reverend Akindayomi though an unlettered man had a number of divine visitation. In one of such visitations, God Himself cast the vision for the church. And obtained a covenant establishing that the church would reach the ends of the earth with total gospel of salvation of Jesus Christ and still be flourishing when Christ shall come in glory. Premise upon this covenant the church started in 1952 as small band of praying men and metamorphosed into evangelical outreach post (to lost souls) called parish.


In early 70, God spoke to Pa Akindayomi about his successor during one of divine visitation. The Lord told him that this man, who was not a member of the church then, would be a young educated man. In 1973, when Enoch Adejare Adeboye joined the church, Rev Akindayomi was able to recognize him in the Spirit as the one that the Lord had spoken about in the past. He prepared necessarily and handed over to him in 1981. He served  fait and righteously to the end.


Pst. Enoch Adejare Adeboye took over the leadership of the church in 1981 as destined by God in the midst of many controversies but God, who called him and destined him, was with him. He forsook all ambition and academic appellation to obey God. His focus was to push God's agenda which is to take the gospel of salvation of Jesus Christ to all nations of the world. There was a revival under this leadership, that resulted in an explosion and exportation of of gospel of Jesus Christ.
