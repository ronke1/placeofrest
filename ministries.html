Our Ministries

Ministries at Place of Rest

Bible Study

We facilitate bible study on thursday evenings. We encourage everyone to come with an open mind as we trust the Spirit of God to help us dig deep into the mind of God, as documented in the Bible.

Choir

We minister to the church in music. We sing a rich collection of Christian worship songs.
We meet once a week for 2 hours.

Evangelism

We are committed to strategically spreading the good news. We intentionally share the news of God's love in the city of Fort Saskatchewan.

Follow Up

We specially welcome first time visitiors in our lounge, and follow up on their interests and walk with God.

Hospitality

We are a small team!
We cater to first time visitors and members on sundays and during special events

Media

We promote the church and her programs on various platforms in the church and online. We are made up of four sub-teams: eNews, Social Media, Livestream, Help & Support

Community Outreach

We plan and implement outreach programs in the city of fort saskatchewan and outside of Canada.

Prayer

We lead prayers during services and special programs. We also take time out to pray for the church and on member requests.

Seniors Team

We meet bi-weekly at the Seniors home at Southforth bend, Fort Sakatchewan. Feel free to reach out to us for more information on how to be a part of the team

Sunday School

We facilitate Sunday School sessions every sunday morning from 9:30am to 10:14am.
2 Tim. 2:15 says Be deligent to present yourself approved to God, a worker who does not need to be ashamed, rightly dividing the word of truth.

Technical

We oversee technical setup for programs and services. Our duties include sound check and monitoring, hardware and software installations, projecting song lyrics, bible texts and messages to the audience, etc.

Ushering

We delight in ushering everyone into the church with a smile. It's our duty to ensure orderliness before and during programs and services. Feel free to reach out to us, we would love ot hear from you

Groups at Place of Rest

Men's Group

We meet periodically to empower each other through prayers, seminars/workshop and networking events.

Women's Group

Women's group comprises of single and married ladies. We organize events periodically to foster a natural bond, as we learn form each other and other field experts.

Young Adults

Our name is HEIRS.
We have quarterly programs specially planned by and run by young adults. We make an effort to express ourselves freely in the confines of Christian virtues and values.

Departments

Facility Management

We are responsible for overseeing smooth operation of church building and the amenities in the church.

Finance

We are a team of professionals that attend to appropriate management of church funds. Your givings to the Church are well documented for tax purposes.

Maintenance

We maintain cleaniness and orderliness in the church.
