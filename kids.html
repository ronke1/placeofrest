Kids @ Place of Rest

Every sunday kids are provided with various fun activities that help with learning more about Christian values and virtues as documented in the bible.

At RCCG Place of Rest Parish, we have different class groups based on ages of the children.

Age Groups

3yrs and under - Baby class

4 yrs - 5 yrs - Junior elementary I

6 yrs - 8 yrs - Junior elementary II

9 yrs - 12 yrs - Senior elementary

13 yrs and above - Highs School

Should you require further clarification or information please do not hesitate to contact us. (7806560320)


Child Registration

A registration form is to be completed by all parents of children attending the RCCG Place of Rest Parish Fort Saskatchewan. Its purpose is to assist the church provide a safe and secure environment for children who participate in our programs or under our care.

Your filing out this form will enable us:

1Officially add your child to the Children Church database

2Inform our teachers about specific health concerns

3Keep you updated on special events that are about to take place
