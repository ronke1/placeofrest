Worship

At Place of rest, we enjoy spirit-filled worship! We come to God's presence with an open heart, looking forward to the warmth of sweet fellowship, as we pour out our love for God in the place of worship.

All our worship selections are in English language, and they cut across various genre of Christian music that align with the gospel of Jesus Christ.

Join us for an amazing time of worship!
