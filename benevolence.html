The Benevolence Group

We connect and care for those in need.

Our vision is to impact lives in the church and our community through acts of kindness and culture of care.

We administer the Benevolence fund which is set up to minister to individuals or families during a time of hardship or crisis by temporarily assisting them with their basic needs (food, clothing, medicals, shelter, utilities or education)

We are here to help! You can confide in us!

You can speak with any of our team members or send an email to porpbenevolence@gmail.com

Roles & Responsibilities

  • Connect with and care for those in need
  • Support in finding employment, resume review, networking with people who may be able to help or connect them with available resources
  • Administer Benevolence Fund
  • Report general state of the fund to Church
  • Maintain confidentiality

Request, Approval & Disbursement

1. Approach

Approach any member of the Benevolence team with the need. A church member who knows any member in need can also reach out to the committee.

2. Apply

Submit an application for financial assistance to the Benevolence team with necessary supporting documentation.

3. Connect

Applicant will be contacted and interviewed by Benevolence team.

4. Decision

Request may be approved, rejected, or additional documents and information may be requested.

5. Communication

Outcome communicated to applicant.
